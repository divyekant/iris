## Bugs Found

### Latent: LIKE Metachar Escaping in Response Times
- **Discovered by**: GC-248
- **File**: `src/api/contacts.rs:283`
- **Issue**: `like_pattern = format!("%{}%", contact_email)` does not escape `%` or `_` SQL wildcards before use in LIKE clause (no ESCAPE clause). An email like `user%test@example.com` could act as a wildcard and match unintended rows.
- **Severity**: Low (correctness, not security — parameterized queries prevent injection)
- **Status**: Not yet fixed
