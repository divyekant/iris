## Bugs Found
- **Category case sensitivity** (GC-030): `ai_category = ?` was case-sensitive. Fixed with `LOWER(ai_category) = LOWER(?)`.
- **Decay validation** (GC-174/175): negative threshold_days and out-of-range decay_factor accepted. Fixed with validation in ai_config.rs.
- **LIKE metachar escaping** (GC-248): `like_pattern = format!("%{}%", contact_email)` doesn't escape `%` or `_` wildcards. Latent correctness bug in contacts.rs:283.
- **confirm_action params_from_iter** (Batch 4 BUG-1): `Vec<Box<dyn ToSql>>` failed with rusqlite params_from_iter. Fixed with `Vec<rusqlite::types::Value>`.
- **Prioritized messages empty** (GC-312/Batch 5 BUG-1): `select_cols` in relationship_priority.rs missing `ai_sentiment`, `ai_needs_reply`, `intent`. Fixed.
- **Social engineering 200/null** (GC-354/Batch 5 BUG-2): `get_social_engineering()` returned 200/null instead of 404 for unanalyzed messages. Fixed.
- **DLP single API key high risk** (GC-362/Batch 5 BUG-3): `determine_risk_level()` classified single ApiKey as High. Fixed to Low.
- **confirm_action UNIQUE constraint** (Batch 4 BUG-1): UPDATE folder='Archive' violated UNIQUE(account_id, message_id, folder). Fixed with `UPDATE OR IGNORE`.
- **Snooze past date accepted** (GC-305): `validate_date()` checks format only, no future-date constraint. Past dates accepted instead of rejected.
