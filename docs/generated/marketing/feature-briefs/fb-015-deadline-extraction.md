# Feature Brief: Deadline Extraction

## One-Liner
Iris finds every deadline buried in your email and puts them where you can actually see them.

## What It Is
Iris reads your incoming emails and automatically surfaces any deadlines mentioned in the text — "report due by Friday," "submit by March 20," "response needed before EOD." Each deadline is extracted into a structured entry with a date and linked back to the original thread. A dashboard widget shows your upcoming deadlines across all accounts in one view.

## Who It's For
- **Primary:** Professionals juggling multiple projects and stakeholders, where deadlines arrive scattered across dozens of threads
- **Secondary:** Teams using email as their primary coordination tool, where missed deadlines have real consequences

## The Problem It Solves
Deadlines don't arrive in a calendar invite — they're buried in the third paragraph of a long email thread. You either catch them when you read carefully, or you miss them entirely. There's no system. You reread old emails to remind yourself what's due. You set manual reminders after every important message. It's friction you shouldn't have to absorb.

## Key Benefits
- **Nothing slips through:** Iris catches deadlines even when they're written casually or buried mid-thread
- **One place for all due dates:** The deadline dashboard aggregates every extracted date across all email accounts
- **Linked to context:** Each deadline links back to the source thread — one click to review what's expected
- **Closure when it's done:** Mark deadlines complete or dismiss them when they're no longer relevant
- **No calendar required:** Works independently of your calendar — deadlines from email, surfaced in email

## How It Works (Simplified)
When a new email arrives, Iris scans the message body for deadline language — explicit dates, relative references like "by Friday," and phrases like "due," "submit," "respond by," or "before." Recognized deadlines are extracted with their resolved date and stored as structured entries. They appear in a dedicated dashboard widget ordered by urgency. You can mark any deadline as complete or dismiss it, and the source thread is always one click away.

## Suggested Messaging
- "Every deadline in your email, surfaced automatically. Never miss another one buried in a thread."
- "Iris finds the due dates so you don't have to reread every email to remember what's due."
- "From inbox to deadline tracker — automatically."
