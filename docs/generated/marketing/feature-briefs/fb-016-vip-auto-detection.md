# Feature Brief: VIP Auto-Detection

## One-Liner
Iris learns who matters most to you and makes sure their emails never get lost in the noise.

## What It Is
Iris continuously analyzes your communication patterns — how often you exchange emails with someone, how quickly you reply, how recently you've been in touch, and how substantive your conversations are. Contacts who score high across these signals automatically get a gold VIP badge in your inbox. You can also manually mark anyone as VIP at any time.

## Who It's For
- **Primary:** Executives, managers, and account owners who communicate with dozens of people and need to ensure key relationships never get deprioritized
- **Secondary:** Anyone who wants their inbox to reflect the actual importance of their relationships, not just recency

## The Problem It Solves
Your inbox treats every sender equally. An email from your most important client sits next to a notification from a tool you barely use. There's no signal for importance — you rely on memory, folder rules you set up years ago, or luck. Important messages get buried and relationships suffer because of it.

## Key Benefits
- **Automatic relationship intelligence:** Iris scores contacts based on how you actually communicate — no manual list-building required
- **Visual priority in the inbox:** Gold VIP badges make high-importance emails immediately visible during triage
- **Always up to date:** Scores update continuously as your communication patterns evolve — new relationships surface, old ones fade
- **Full manual control:** Override the algorithm instantly — mark anyone VIP or remove the designation whenever you choose
- **Relationship awareness without CRM overhead:** Get the signal of a contact management system without leaving your email client

## How It Works (Simplified)
Iris builds a communication graph from your email history, scoring each contact across four dimensions: how frequently you exchange emails, how consistently you reply to them, how recently you've been in touch, and the depth of your conversations. Contacts that rank high across these signals are flagged as VIPs. Their emails display a gold badge in the inbox so they stand out immediately. The scoring runs in the background and adjusts over time as your relationships change.
