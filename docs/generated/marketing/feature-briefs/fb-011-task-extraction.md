# Task Extraction

## One-Liner
Every action item buried in your email, surfaced automatically — with priority and deadline included.

## What It Is
Task Extraction reads your emails and pulls out every action item, commitment, and follow-up request. Each extracted task includes a description of what needs to be done, a priority level, and a deadline if one was mentioned. No highlighting, no manual copy-paste — Iris does the reading for you.

## Who It's For
Professionals whose to-do list lives in their inbox. Project managers, team leads, consultants, and anyone who regularly receives emails containing requests, next steps, or commitments they're expected to act on.

## The Problem It Solves
Email is where work gets assigned, but it was never designed to be a task manager. Action items are buried in paragraphs, buried in threads, buried in forwarded chains. You read an email, mentally note what you need to do, and hours later that note is gone. Task Extraction means you don't have to manually convert emails into tasks — Iris does it for you, instantly.

## Key Benefits
- **Nothing slips through.** Iris catches every action item in an email, not just the ones you notice.
- **Priority built in.** Each task comes labeled with a priority level so you know what to tackle first.
- **Deadlines surfaced.** When a date or timeframe is mentioned, Iris captures it with the task.
- **Inbox to to-do list in seconds.** Turn any email thread into a structured list of next steps immediately.
- **Less cognitive load.** Stop holding tasks in your head while you read — Iris holds them for you.

## How It Works (Simplified)
Open an email thread in Iris and trigger Task Extraction. Iris reads the thread and presents a structured list of action items — each with a clear description, a priority, and any deadline mentioned in the email. Review the list and act.
