# Thread Notes

## One-Liner
Add private, persistent notes to any email thread — context that stays with the conversation, not scattered across your memory.

## What It Is
Thread Notes lets you attach private sticky notes to any email thread inside Iris. These notes live alongside the conversation, are completely private (never sent to anyone), and persist so you always have the context you need when you return to a thread.

## Who It's For
Anyone who uses email for ongoing relationships, projects, or negotiations — and has ever returned to a thread wishing they'd written something down. Account managers, recruiters, project leads, consultants, and anyone who juggles multiple active conversations at once.

## The Problem It Solves
Email is where decisions get made, but the context around those decisions lives in your head. You remember a call, a concern, a commitment — but when the thread picks back up a week later, that context is gone. Notes apps don't connect to emails. Sticky notes get lost. Thread Notes keeps your private context exactly where you need it: inside the conversation.

## Key Benefits
- **Never lose context.** Notes live inside the thread, not in a separate app you have to search later.
- **Completely private.** Your notes are yours — they stay on your device and are never visible to anyone else.
- **Always ready.** Return to any thread and your notes are right there, no searching required.
- **Lightweight.** No new habits to build — just write a note while you're reading and move on.
- **Meeting-ready.** Prep for calls by leaving yourself reminders inside the exact thread you'll reference.

## How It Works (Simplified)
Open any email thread in Iris. Click the notes area and type whatever you need — a reminder, a summary, an action item. Iris saves it privately and displays it every time you open that thread. No syncing, no sharing, no extra apps.

## Competitive Context
Requires additional context.

## Proof Points
- Notes are stored locally and never transmitted — no privacy tradeoff for the benefit.
- Persists across sessions: close Iris, reopen it, your notes are still there.
- Tied to the thread, not the individual message — so the note stays relevant as the conversation evolves.
