# Feature Brief: Follow-Up Reminders

## One-Liner
Iris knows when you're waiting for a reply — and reminds you before it's too late to follow up.

## What It Is
Iris monitors your sent emails and identifies threads where you're waiting on a response that hasn't come. When a thread goes quiet longer than expected, Iris surfaces it as a follow-up reminder with an urgency level and a suggested follow-up date. You can snooze, dismiss, or jump straight into the thread to send a nudge — without having to track anything manually.

## Who It's For
- **Primary:** Sales professionals, account managers, recruiters, and anyone whose work depends on getting responses from other people
- **Secondary:** Managers and team leads who send action items and need visibility into what's still open

## The Problem It Solves
You send an important email and wait. Days pass. You forget which threads are still open, who you're waiting on, and how long it's been. You either follow up too late — when the moment has passed — or you don't follow up at all and deals stall, projects slip, and relationships cool. Managing this manually means re-reading your Sent folder regularly. Nobody does that.

## Key Benefits
- **Never lose a thread in your sent folder:** Iris tracks outbound emails that need a reply so you don't have to
- **Urgency signals at a glance:** Each reminder carries an urgency level so you know which follow-ups to prioritize today
- **Suggested timing:** Iris recommends when to follow up based on context — not just a flat reminder, but the right time
- **One-click action:** Jump directly from the reminder into the thread to send your follow-up without hunting for it
- **Flexible snooze and dismiss:** Not ready to follow up yet? Snooze it. No longer relevant? Dismiss it. You stay in control

## How It Works (Simplified)
Iris monitors your sent messages and identifies emails that requested a response, contained an action item, or asked a question. When a thread goes quiet past the expected response window, Iris flags it as a pending follow-up. Each reminder surfaces in a dedicated view with the thread subject, how long it's been waiting, an urgency level, and a suggested follow-up date. You can open the thread directly, snooze the reminder for a specific period, or dismiss it if it's no longer relevant. The system updates continuously — when a reply arrives, the reminder disappears automatically.

## Suggested Messaging
- "Iris knows what you sent and who hasn't replied. Follow-up reminders that actually work."
- "Stop re-reading your Sent folder. Iris tracks your open threads and tells you when it's time to follow up."
- "Never let a deal, a decision, or a deadline stall because someone didn't reply. Iris has you covered."
