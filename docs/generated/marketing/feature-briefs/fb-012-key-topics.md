# Key Topics

## One-Liner
Understand what you actually talk about with any contact — and who you talk to most.

## What It Is
Key Topics analyzes your email conversations with any contact and surfaces the recurring themes — the subjects, projects, and concerns that come up most often. Results are cached after the first analysis so subsequent lookups are instant. Alongside topic analysis, Iris ranks your top contacts by email frequency, giving you a clear picture of your most active relationships.

## Who It's For
Relationship-driven professionals who manage multiple ongoing partnerships. Account executives, business development leaders, client success managers, recruiters, and anyone who needs to walk into a meeting with sharp context on who they're talking to and what matters to that person.

## The Problem It Solves
When you email someone regularly, you build up an implicit understanding of what drives your conversations — but it's all in your head, accumulated slowly over months. Before a call or a meeting, you scramble to re-read old emails for context. Key Topics gives you that understanding on demand, without the scrambling.

## Key Benefits
- **Instant relationship context.** See the core themes of any relationship before you get on a call.
- **Meeting prep in seconds.** Know what matters to a contact without re-reading months of email.
- **Prioritize your relationships.** The top contacts list shows you who you're most actively connected to at a glance.
- **Spot patterns you'd otherwise miss.** Recurring topics you've never consciously noticed become visible.
- **Fast after the first look.** Results are cached, so the second lookup is instant.

## How It Works (Simplified)
Open a contact's profile in Iris and view their Key Topics. Iris analyzes your conversation history and presents the most frequent themes — topics you've discussed repeatedly across all your emails together. The top contacts panel gives you a ranked view of your most active relationships by volume.
