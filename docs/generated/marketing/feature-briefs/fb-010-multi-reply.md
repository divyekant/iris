# Multi-Option Reply

## One-Liner
Three ready-to-send reply drafts, three different tones — pick the one that fits and move on.

## What It Is
Multi-Option Reply uses AI to write three distinct reply drafts for any email: one formal, one casual, and one brief. You choose the version that matches the situation, use it as-is or tweak it, and send. You can also give Iris optional guidance — like "decline politely" or "ask for a deadline extension" — to make the options more specific to what you need.

## Who It's For
Anyone who spends too long thinking about how to phrase a reply. Sales reps, customer-facing teams, managers handling sensitive requests, and professionals who switch communication styles across different relationships throughout the day.

## The Problem It Solves
Most emails don't need a carefully crafted reply — they just need the right reply. But "right" means different things for different people and situations. Too formal and you seem cold. Too casual and you seem unprofessional. Too long and nobody reads it. Multi-Option Reply removes the blank-page problem and the tone-matching guesswork in one step.

## Key Benefits
- **No more staring at a blank reply box.** Three solid starting points appear immediately.
- **Tone-matched for every relationship.** Formal, casual, and brief — you choose what fits the moment.
- **Guided drafts when you need them.** Add optional context and Iris adjusts all three options to match your intent.
- **Edit or send as-is.** Each draft is yours to refine or send directly.
- **Consistent output under pressure.** Works just as well on the tenth email of the day as it does on the first.

## How It Works (Simplified)
While reading an email, click Multi-Reply. Iris generates three drafts — formal, casual, brief — instantly. Optionally add a note like "push back on the timeline" and Iris incorporates that into all three. Select the version you want, edit if needed, and send.
