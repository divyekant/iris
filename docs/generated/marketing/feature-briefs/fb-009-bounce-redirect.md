# Bounce / Redirect

## One-Liner
Send the right email to the right person — with the original sender's name intact, so nothing gets lost in translation.

## What It Is
Bounce (also called Redirect) lets you forward an email to someone else while preserving the original sender's name and address. The recipient sees the message as if it was sent to them directly from the original author — not wrapped in a forwarded chain from you.

## Who It's For
Managers and team leads who route inbound requests, operations teams handling customer or vendor email, executive assistants who triage their principal's inbox, and anyone who regularly delegates email to the right person.

## The Problem It Solves
Standard forwarding wraps the original message in a new email from you, burying the original context and making it look like a handoff. The person who receives it has to dig through the chain to understand what happened. Worse, when they reply, it often goes back to you instead of the original sender. Bounce solves this: the email arrives clean, with the original sender front and center, so the recipient can reply directly without any confusion.

## Key Benefits
- **Clean handoffs.** The recipient gets the original email as-is — no forwarding clutter, no nested chains.
- **Reply goes to the right place.** Because the original sender info is preserved, replies go directly back to them.
- **One click.** Delegating an email takes seconds, not a compose session.
- **No context lost.** The recipient sees exactly what the original sender sent, without any middleman noise.
- **Routing at scale.** Makes email delegation a repeatable, professional process for entire teams.

## How It Works (Simplified)
Open an email in Iris, choose Redirect, and enter the recipient's address. Iris delivers the original message to them with the original sender's name and address preserved. From the recipient's perspective, it looks like it was sent to them directly.
