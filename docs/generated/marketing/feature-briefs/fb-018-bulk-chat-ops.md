# Feature Brief: Bulk Chat Operations

## One-Liner
Tell Iris what to do with your email in plain English — and it handles the rest.

## What It Is
Now you can manage your inbox through conversation. Open the Iris AI Chat panel, describe what you want done — "archive all emails from LinkedIn," "mark everything from last week as read," "move all newsletters to the updates category" — and Iris identifies exactly what will be affected, shows you a preview, and asks for your confirmation before acting. Bulk operations that used to take minutes of clicking happen in seconds through chat.

## Who It's For
- **Primary:** Inbox overload sufferers — anyone sitting on hundreds or thousands of unread emails who wants to bulk-clean without tedious manual selection
- **Secondary:** Power users who do regular inbox maintenance and want a faster, more natural interface than filter rules or keyboard shortcuts

## The Problem It Solves
Bulk email management is painful. You select messages one by one, or build complex filter rules, or just give up and leave the mess. Even when you know exactly what you want to do — "get rid of everything from that vendor" — the tools make it feel like work. You end up doing nothing, and the inbox grows.

## Key Benefits
- **Natural language control:** No rules, no filters, no multi-select clicking — just describe what you want done
- **Always a preview first:** Iris shows you exactly which emails will be affected before executing — no accidental mass-deletions
- **Wide action coverage:** Archive, mark read/unread, trash, star, move to category — all available through chat
- **Fast inbox cleanup:** Operations that take 20 minutes of clicking take 20 seconds through conversation
- **Undo-safe by design:** Confirmation step before every bulk action means you stay in control

## How It Works (Simplified)
In the Iris AI Chat panel, you type what you want done in plain English. Iris interprets your request, queries your email to identify the affected messages, and presents a summary — "I found 47 emails from LinkedIn. Here's what I'll do: archive all of them." You confirm, and the operation runs instantly across all matching messages. Iris supports archiving, marking read or unread, moving to trash, starring, and reassigning to any category. Every bulk action requires explicit confirmation before it executes.
