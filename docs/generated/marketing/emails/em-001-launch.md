# Email Announcement: Iris Launch

**Subject**: AI email intelligence that never leaves your machine

---

AI email tools are powerful. But they all require your data to leave your machine.

Iris is a different approach: a self-hosted email client with full AI intelligence that runs entirely on your hardware. No cloud. No telemetry. No tradeoffs.

**What Iris does:**

- Automatically classifies, prioritizes, and extracts key info from every email
- Semantic search: find emails by meaning, not just keywords
- AI chat: ask questions about your email, get answers with citations
- Writing assist: rewrite, formalize, shorten -- all locally
- Agent API: let AI agents search, read, draft, and send with scoped permissions
- Works with Gmail, Outlook, and any IMAP provider

**What Iris doesn't do:**

- Send your data anywhere
- Charge a subscription fee
- Lock you into one email provider

Deploy with one command: `docker compose up`

Built with Rust, Svelte 5, SQLite, and Ollama. Open source. Free.
