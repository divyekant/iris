---

## Solution

### Iris gives you both

**Local AI Intelligence**
Every email is automatically classified, prioritized, and analyzed by AI running on your machine through Ollama. Categories, sentiment, key dates, amounts, and action items -- surfaced instantly. Correct a mistake, and Iris learns.

**Semantic Search**
Find emails by meaning, not just keywords. Search for "emails about the budget revision" and find results even when those exact words never appear. Powered by a local vector store with automatic full-text fallback.

**Conversational AI Chat**
Ask questions about your email in plain English. "What did Sarah say about the timeline?" Get answers with citations linking to source emails. Propose actions like drafting a reply -- with your explicit approval before anything happens.

**Open Agent API**
Give AI agents secure access to your email. Search, read, draft, and send through a REST API with four permission levels, SHA-256 key security, and full audit logging. Build email automation on your infrastructure.

## How It Works

### Three steps to AI-powered email

**1. Deploy**
Run `docker compose up`. Iris and a local AI runtime start in under two minutes. No infrastructure team required.

**2. Connect**
Add your email accounts -- Gmail and Outlook via OAuth2, any other provider via IMAP. Iris syncs your inbox and starts AI processing immediately.

**3. Work Smarter**
Open your inbox to find emails pre-classified and prioritized. Search by meaning. Ask the AI questions. Let agents automate workflows. Everything stays on your machine.
